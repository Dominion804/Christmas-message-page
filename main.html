Say this prayer!

Say this prayer:

"Dear Jesus, I accept what You did for me, and I am ready to start living for You today. I am ready to become righteous in the sight of God through Your sacrifice and to make You LORD. Take over, LORD."

Congratulations, you are now a child of God.

Now, just one more thing. In order to grow in this faith, you need to fellowship with other believers so you can encourage each other (Heb. 10:25). So, find a local church, regularly attend, and serve God there with all your heart. Find something you can do for God in the church that will help it grow.
